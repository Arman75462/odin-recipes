Shrimp

Beautiful, tasty butter garlic shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for some heat)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine the melted butter, minced garlic, lemon juice, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  2. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the bowl and toss them in the garlic butter mixture, ensuring they are well coated. Let it marinate for about 10 minutes.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the shrimp in a single layer, along with the marinade. Cook the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side until they turn pink and opaque. Be careful not to overcook them as shrimp cook quickly.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat and transfer the cooked shrimp to a serving dish.
  5. Optional: Garnish with freshly chopped parsley for some extra color and flavor.
