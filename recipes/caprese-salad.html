Honey Mustard Chicken

Beautiful, tasty caprese salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 1 ball of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze or balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Wash the tomatoes and basil leaves thoroughly. Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into even, thick slices.
  2. On a serving platter or individual plates, arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices in an alternating pattern, slightly overlapping each other.
  3. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  4. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the salad, making sure to coat the tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil evenly.
  5. Drizzle a balsamic glaze or balsamic vinegar over the salad. The sweet tanginess of the balsamic glaze complements the flavors of the tomatoes and mozzarella perfectly.
  6. Season the salad with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper according to your taste.
