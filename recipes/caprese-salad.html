Honey Mustard Chicken

Beautiful, tasty honey mustard chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme (or any other dried herbs you prefer)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together Dijon mustard, honey, olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  3. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish or a lined baking sheet.
  4. Pour the honey mustard mixture over the chicken, ensuring each piece is evenly coated.
  5. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C).
  6. Optional: If desired, you can broil the chicken for an additional 1-2 minutes to give it a slightly golden and caramelized finish.
  7. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a minute or two.
  8. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for added freshness and presentation.
